# Junior Data Analyst – Hypetap Pty Ltd

**Company:** Hypetap Pty Ltd  
**Location:** Melbourne, VIC, Australia (Hybrid)  
**Source:** SEEK Job Posting  
**Link:** https://www.seek.com.au/

---

## Job Summary
Hypetap, Australia’s leading influencer marketing agency, is seeking a Junior Data Analyst to analyse marketing and social media data. The role focuses on extracting insights from datasets, building industry benchmarks, and supporting business growth through data-driven strategies.

---

## Key Responsibilities
- Extract and analyse data from data warehouses using SQL and analytics tools  
- Prepare reports highlighting key performance indicators for clients  
- Develop industry benchmarks and performance standards  
- Support sales and client teams with analytical insights  
- Assist in industry reporting and PR initiatives  
- Ensure data accuracy, consistency, and quality  

---

## Required Skills
- SQL querying and data analysis skills  
- Strong analytical and problem-solving ability  
- Data reporting and visualisation skills  
- Communication and presentation skills  
- Interest in social media analytics and marketing data  

---

## Experience / Qualifications
- 1–2 years experience in a data analysis role preferred  
- Bachelor’s degree in quantitative field (Statistics, Engineering, Economics, etc.)  
- Knowledge of ETL processes and data warehousing  
- Python or Tableau experience desirable  
- Awareness of AI/ML concepts advantageous  

---

## Additional Notes
The company offers flexible work practices, career development opportunities, and exposure to global clients. A technical test may be part of the interview process.
