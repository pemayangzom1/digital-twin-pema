# Graduate Analyst – Advisory – Hatch

**Company:** Hatch  
**Location:** Brisbane, Queensland, Australia  
**Source:** Company Job Posting / Graduate Advisory Analyst Role  

---

## Job Summary
Hatch is a global advisory, engineering, and consulting company focused on mining, energy, and infrastructure industries. The Graduate Analyst – Advisory role supports consulting projects by conducting analysis, reporting, financial modelling, and data-driven business insights to help clients achieve technical and strategic objectives.

---

## Key Responsibilities
- Analyse business performance indicators, operational costs, and project metrics  
- Identify, rank, and manage project risks  
- Develop financial models to support investment decisions  
- Research, clean, analyse, and interpret datasets  
- Prepare client presentations, reports, spreadsheets, and proposals  
- Work across diverse consulting projects and industries  
- Support client engagement and advisory delivery activities  
- Participate in project meetings and potentially travel to project sites  
- Develop consulting, analytical, and project delivery skills  

---

## Required Skills
- Strong analytical and problem-solving abilities  
- Data analysis skills including Excel, SQL, or similar tools  
- Ability to work with large datasets  
- Critical thinking and attention to detail  
- Strong written and verbal communication skills  
- Ability to work collaboratively in consulting teams  

---

## Qualifications / Experience
- Undergraduate degree in Commerce, Business Administration, Engineering, Economics, or related field  
- Interest in consulting, analytics, or advisory services  
- Ability to learn quickly and adapt in professional environments  

---

## Additional Notes
This graduate role offers exposure to advisory consulting projects across infrastructure, energy, and mining industries, focusing on analytics, financial modelling, and strategic decision support.
