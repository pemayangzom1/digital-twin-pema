## Job Summary
BNK is hiring a Graduate Data Analyst to work closely with business leaders and the Data Analytics team to deliver insights, dashboards, and reporting solutions. The role combines technical analytics skills (SQL + Power BI) with a consultative approach to help stakeholders make data-driven decisions.

---

## Key Responsibilities
- Design, build, and maintain dashboards and reporting for internal stakeholders  
- Ensure analytics outputs are scalable, accurate, and aligned with business requirements  
- Adhere to and improve analytics standards and reporting processes  
- Act as an analytics partner to a business unit and build strong stakeholder relationships  
- Communicate insights clearly to both technical and non-technical audiences  
- Write SQL queries to extract, join, and wrangle data from multiple sources
