## Job Summary

Canstar is seeking a Graduate Research Analyst to join its Research Department within the Ratings Team. The role focuses on market research, data analysis, consumer insights, and ratings development in the financial services industry. This position provides early-career professionals an opportunity to build expertise in analytics, financial product research, and media insights while collaborating with industry professionals.

- Support development of ratings, reports, and research outputs  
- Analyse datasets to generate insights for consumer decision-making  
- Assist with media insights and research communication  
- Collaborate with research teams to maintain accuracy and data integrity  
- Produce clear written and analytical reports for stakeholders
